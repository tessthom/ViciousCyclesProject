Who We Are

Who We Are:

The Vicious Cycles crew is made up of local folks who genuinely want to make the Triangle Area a safer, more fun and inclusive place for cyclists of all kinds.

Some of us were born and breaded in Durham and some hail from other countries entirely. We're all about coming together, no matter our differences, for the love of cycling.

We fully recognize that the world of cycling has historically been dominated and thereby gatekept by normative forces of class, race and gender. It is our intention to put the work into breaking down those barriers, making sure folks of all indentities and incomes have equitable access to dependable wheels and a safe cycling experience.

Every month we donate 15% of our gear sales to a different local organization doing good in our community, with a focus on harm reduction and supporting marginalized groups. We host a Parts Swap & Donation Meet to increase access to reliable gear and maintain a Pay It Forward program at checkout for anyone interested. Check out our Events page for more.

If you're interested in joining the crew, teaching a class, providing feedback or have questions about anything we're up to, just give us a ring or stop on by. You can find our details back over in Contacts. We've got cold sodas, seltzers and local craft beer up for grabs in a cooler gifted from our buddies over at Dain's Place, too!
